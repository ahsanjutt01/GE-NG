ABOUT COMPANY

Our story began in the late 1994s when we saw the steadily growing mountains of plastic and used beverage cartons in Pakistan. We planned to start first recycling program to collect domestic waste from houses, sort them and recycle waste to be used to manufacture the products.

A winning combination of material & technology, made from unique blend of recycled polyethene plastic such as shopping bags & plastic scraps

Strong & impact resistant with the beauty of natural wood, it is maintenance free with a long lasting life

The perfect mix of recycled materials make the product water proof, resists rotting, splitting & splintering

Removes the menace of plastic bags from mainstream thus conserving the already dwindling forest reserves

  • Loving Mother Nature

HOW WE RECYCLE
