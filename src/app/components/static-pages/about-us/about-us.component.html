ABOUT US

OUR JOURNEY

Our story began in the late 1994s when we saw the steadily growing mountains of plastic and used beverage cartons in Pakistan. We planned to start first recycling program to collect domestic waste from houses, sort them and recycle waste to be used to manufacture the products.

COMPANY VALUES

Our goal is to deliver a quality product our customers want in a timely fashion. To achieve this vision, we developed state-of-the-art technology and lean manufacturing processes that have minimized our waste stream and ensured our products are built quickly and accurately.
